Welcome to Novel Vibrations

Novel Vibrations is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to empowering Atlanta's adult houseless population through art therapy and entrepreneurial opportunities. Inspired by the success of the "Living Museum" model, we believe in the healing power of creativity to foster mental health, develop skills, and create pathways to self-sufficiency.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a supportive and nurturing environment where individuals facing mental health challenges and homelessness can explore their creativity, develop new skills, and find pathways to healing and self-sufficiency. We focus our efforts in Atlanta's Little Five Points and Reynoldstown neighborhoods, offering free art therapy workshops, entrepreneurial training, and opportunities for our participants to showcase and sell their artwork.

The Power of Creativity

At Novel Vibrations, we believe that artistic expression has the potential to transform lives. Through our programs, participants can:

  • Express themselves in a safe, non-judgmental environment
  • Develop valuable artistic and business skills
  • Build self-esteem and confidence
  • Connect with others and reduce isolation
  • Work through trauma and emotional challenges
  • Generate income through the sale of their artwork
  • Work towards self-sufficiency and improved mental health

Our Approach

Our approach is based on several key principles:

  • Non-judgmental acceptance: We create a safe space where individuals can express themselves freely without fear of criticism or judgment.
  • Skill development: We provide training in various artistic techniques and entrepreneurial skills to empower our participants.
  • Community building: We foster a sense of belonging and connection among our participants, staff, and volunteers.
  • Holistic support: We recognize that creativity is just one aspect of healing, and we connect our participants with additional resources for housing, healthcare, and other essential needs.
  • Empowerment: We encourage our participants to take ownership of their creative journey and personal growth.
